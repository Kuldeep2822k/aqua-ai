## Engagement Strategies

### High-Impact Threads (Reddit)

- Use "Show & Tell" format.
- Lead with the _problem_ (water safety in India) then the _solution_ (Aqua-AI).
- Include high-quality screenshots/gifs of the map visualization.

### Technical Deep-Dives (Dev.to / Hashnode)

- Focus on the integration of government APIs.
- Explain the AI modeling approach (TensorFlow/Python).
- Detail the frontend architecture (React + Leaflet).

### Social Impact (LinkedIn / r/India)

- Focus on the "Made in India" and "For Public Good" aspects.
- Use emotional storytelling about the scale of the water pollution problem.
