# Initial Concept
AI-powered water quality monitoring platform for India.

# Product Guide: Aqua-AI

## Vision
To provide India's 1.4 billion people with real-time, AI-driven insights into water quality, ensuring public health safety and sustainable environmental management.

## Target Users
- **Indian Public:** To stay informed about water safety and receive pollution alerts.
- **Government Agencies:** For policy-making, resource management, and regional monitoring.
- **Industrial Departments:** To monitor compliance and manage industrial waste impact.

## Primary Goals
- **Real-time Monitoring:** Fully operational integration with CPCB and Ministry of Jal Shakti for live water quality tracking.
- **Predictive Analytics:** Using machine learning to forecast pollution events before they impact communities.
- **Public Health Protection:** Delivering actionable safety recommendations and timely risk alerts.

## Core Features
- **Interactive Maps:** Real-time visualization of water bodies across India with color-coded pollution levels.
- **Smart Risk Alerts:** Automated notifications ranging from "Safe" to "Critical" based on water parameters.
- **Historical Trends:** In-depth analysis of BOD, TDS, pH, and other critical water quality indicators.

## Platform Scope
- **Phased Expansion:** Initial focus on major rivers (Ganga, Yamuna) and critical industrial hubs, with a roadmap for Pan-India coverage using all available government APIs.
