# Tech Stack: Aqua-AI

## Frontend
- **React 18 & TypeScript:** Providing a modern, type-safe foundations for our user interface.
- **Leaflet/Mapbox:** Powering our interactive map and geospatial visualizations.
- **Tailwind CSS & Radix UI:** Ensuring a consistent, responsive, and accessible design system.

## Backend
- **Node.js & Express.js:** A robust and scalable foundation for our RESTful API services.
- **JWT Authentication:** Securing user data and ensuring authorized access.
- **Winston Logger:** Providing structured logging for better monitoring and debugging.

## Database & Storage
- **PostgreSQL & PostGIS:** Our primary store for relational and spatial data.
- **Redis:** Used for caching and session management to optimize performance.
- **SQLite:** Employed for local development and testing environments.
- **Knex ORM:** Providing a flexible and powerful interface for database operations.

## AI & ML Pipeline
- **Python:** The core language for our data processing and machine learning workflows.
- **Data Pipeline (ETL):** Automated Python collectors for CPCB and Ministry APIs with a centralized normalization engine.
- **TensorFlow & Scikit-Learn:** Powering our predictive modeling and pollution event forecasting.
- **Pandas & NumPy:** Ensuring efficient data manipulation and analysis.

## Infrastructure & DevOps
- **Docker & Docker Compose:** Containerizing our services for consistency across environments.
- **GitHub Actions (CI/CD):** Automating our build, test, and deployment pipelines.
