# Product Guidelines: Aqua-AI

## Prose Style
- **Technical & Precise:** Our content focuses on clarity and technical accuracy. We provide specific data points and avoid ambiguity to ensure experts and stakeholders can rely on our insights.

## Branding & Visual Identity
- **Minimalist & Modern:** We prioritize simplicity and a clean aesthetic, reducing clutter to focus on the data.
- **Data-Driven & Visual:** Our identity is centered around data visualization. We use charts, maps, and metrics to tell the story of India's water quality.
- **Environmental & Clean:** Our color palette (Blue, White, Green) reflects the natural environment and inspires trust in our mission for clean water.

## User Experience (UX) Principles
- **Inclusive & Accessible:** We are committed to WCAG compliance, ensuring the platform is usable for everyone, regardless of their ability or background.
- **Interactive & Responsive:** The platform provides immediate feedback for every user action, creating a seamless and engaging experience across all devices.

## Communication Strategy
- **Empowering & Helpful:** When communicating risk, we focus on providing users with the knowledge and actionable steps they need to stay safe and protect their communities.
